Frontier AI bulletin - 13/05/24 to 26/05/24

Created on May 26, 2024

2024   ·   frontier-ai-bulletin

Industry

  • 26/05/24: iOS 18 AI rumours are fairly tame following announcement of Apple<>OpenAI partnership and ahead of Apple’s WWDC 24 on June 10th
  • 26/05/24: xAI announce a $6bn series B funding round
  • 25/05/24: Google’s AI Overview feature got ribbed a bit for saying silly things
  • 24/05/24: OpenAI release most employees from non-disparagement agreements
  • 23/05/24: Anthropic comment on their CSAM policy
  • 22/05/24: Google team share framework for classifying levels of AGI
  • 21/05/24: Anthropic share results showing they can identify internal model states corresponding to specific concepts in Sonnet, and that forcing these states when using the model changes its behaviour
  • 22/05/24: Nvidia records quarterly revenue of $26bn, 362% of what it was a year ago
  • 22/05/24: TikTok announce they’ll soon integrate lots of generative tools for advertisers on its platform
  • 21/05/24: Microsoft add a multimodal to its small/on-device Phi 3 model family
  • 21/05/24: Microsoft run a small exclusive-access preview of Copilot agents, indicate they’ll be available in public preview later this year
  • 21/05/24: OpenAI blog post responding to Seoul AI safety summit
  • 20/05/24: Anthropic share blog post Reflections on Our Responsible Scaling Policy
  • 20/05/24: ChatGPT app earns $4.2m in net revenue over four days following GPT-4o announcement
  • 20/05/24: Microsoft announce Copilot+ PC, which introduces a tool for searching everything that’s been displayed on-screen and relies on a mixture of on-device and cloud calls to LLMs (also has an ms paint image generation upgrade <3, and a “Copilot key”)
  • 17/05/24: Slack trains its models on user data by default, opt-out necessary if you don’t want that
  • 17/05/24: Google share their Frontier Safety Framework
  • 15/05/24: Google demo seeing and talking agent project Astra, blog post
  • 15/05/24: OpenAI Chief Scientist Ilya Sutskever and Superalignment team lead Jan Leike depart the company
  • 15/05/24: Stability in sales talks, alleged first quarter $5m in and $30mn out
  • 14/05/24: Google announce Gemini Nano will be integrated into Chrome desktop in next update
  • 14/05/24: Google announce text-to-video model Veo and text-to-image Imagen 3 (second link includes demos from artists using Google’s unreleased Music AI Sandbox)
  • 14/05/24: Google announce lower-latency and lower-cost Gemini 1.5 Pro iteration Flash
  • 14/05/24: Google reminds everyone about SynthID media watermarking technique alongside their keynotes on new video, image, and text synthesis models
  • 13/05/24: OpenAI bring more tools to ChatGPT, including web browsing and memory
  • 13/05/24: OpenAI announce GPT-4o, which subsequently ranks first on LMSYS chatbot leaderboard
  • 13/05/24: Alibaba investing in China’s leading AI startups (Moonshot, Zhipu, Minimax, 01.ai), pundits speculate it’s trying to position itself as premier China AI compute provider

Open-source

  • 26/05/24: Github trending for the last week lists fabric (bag of LLM-driven CLI utilities), web-llm, (in-browser LLM API wrapper), Perplexica (Perplexity clone), WrenAI (text-to-SQL), screenshot-to-code, khoj (open-source chatbot), Verba (RAG chatbot), farfalle (Perplexity clone, again)
  • 23/05/24: Cohere open-weights release of new multilingual LLM family Aya 23
  • 14/05/24: Google announce additional Gemma models now (inc PaliGemma, a multimodal Gemma model) and Gemma 2 in June

Academia, Civil Society, and Not-for-profits

  • 26/05/24: AI Incidents Database contains 681 entries, most recently “Las Vegas self-driving bus involved in accident”, “Uber autonomous vehicle killed pedestrian in Arizona”, “Google’s YouTube Kids app presents inappropriate content”
  • 26/05/24: AIAAIC database contains 1495 entries, most recent are “eight newspapers sue OpenAI and Microsoft for copyright infringement”, “Singapore writers resist government plan to train AI using their work”, “Hoodline use of AI to generate news solicits backlash”
  • 22/05/24: MLCommons share a draft of their test specification schema
  • 17/05/24: Epoch publish paper Do the Returns to Software R&D Point Towards a Singularity?
  • 16/05/24: METR is hiring engineers to build automated improvement evals
  • 14/05/24: Concordia AI share report State of AI Safety in China
  • ??/05/24: Centre for Security and Emerging Technology share a translation of draft China AI law

News Outlets and Blogs

  • 18/05/24: The Guardian write about China synthetic news anchors

Governments

  • 21/05/24: Seoul Declaration is signed at Seoul AI safety summit, reiterates commitments of Bletchley Declaration, major global AI companies - new inclusions from China and UAE - sign up to Frontier AI Safety Commitments
  • 21/05/24: European Council formally adopt the EU AI Act
  • 20/05/24: UK Automated Vehicle Act becomes law
  • 20/05/24: UK AISI share a report on their evaluations and a fourth progress report
  • 20/05/24: Inaugural International Scientific Report on the Safety of Advanced AI is published to accompany the Seoul summit
  • 17/05/24: EU threatens to fine Microsoft 1% of global turnover for not responding adequately to their DSA-based request for information relating to risk from Bing AI features
  • 15/05/24: US and China envoys meet in Geneva for first 1-1 to discuss AI risk; US irritated by misinformation, China annoyed by export restrictions and advocate for UN lead in international AI safety coordination
  • 06/05/24: The US National AI Research Resource announced the first 35 projects to be awarded compute and model access
