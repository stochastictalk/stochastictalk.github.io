 Hello! I'm Jerome. 
 
 I work at the [Artificial Intelligence Safety Institute](https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/ai-safety-institute/about) as a Research Strategy & Delivery Manager. Before I joined AISI, I worked as a data scientist for five years, most recently at [Faculty](https://faculty.ai/).

 I deliver high-pressure projects at the intersection of artificial intelligence and national security. I do this by coordinating and enabling teams to build software and procedures for measuring the capabilities and impacts of AI systems such as GPT-4 and PaLM. I'm motivated by the thrill of the chase and creating a stable and enjoyable future for everyone.
