 I currently work in London at the [Artificial Intelligence Safety Institute](https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/ai-safety-institute/about). Before I joined AISI, I worked as a data scientist for five years, most recently at [Faculty](https://faculty.ai/).

 I deliver high-pressure projects at the intersection of artificial intelligence and national security. I do this by coordinating and enabling teams to build software and procedures for measuring the capabilities and impacts of AI systems such as GPT-4 and PaLM. I also write about the impacts of technology. 
 
If you are in London and would like a coffee, you can say hello to me via this form.
