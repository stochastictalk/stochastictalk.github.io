Jerome Wynne

Research engineer at Prolific, working on methods for evaluating large language models. Before Prolific I spent two years at the UK AI Security Institute, latterly in the Science of Evaluations group. Earlier I was a data scientist at Faculty.

I studied statistics at Imperial College London — an MSc, then a year of a PhD — after reading design engineering at Bristol.
